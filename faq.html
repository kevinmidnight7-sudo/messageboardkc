Frequently Asked Questions

Winning the Overall Winner title is all about skill, consistency, and fair play. To be crowned as the Overall Winner at the end of a KC Event:

  • Play by the rules: You must not have broken any event rules throughout the game. Any form of cheating, exploiting, or breaking the event guidelines will automatically disqualify you.
  • Be at the top when it counts: You must be #1 on the leaderboard when the game ends. It’s not just about leading early on — you need to maintain your position all the way to the finish.
  • Play the full event: You need to have participated for the entire duration of the event. If you join halfway through, even if you climb to the top of the leaderboard, you won’t be eligible for the Overall Winner title.

In short, it’s about dedication and fair competition — stay active, follow the rules, and secure your position until the very end to earn the crown.

Yes, you must have a KC Events account to be eligible for titles like Best Offence or Best Defence. This is to ensure:

  • Fair tracking of wins: Having an account allows us to accurately track your achievements and maintain a transparent system for awarding titles.

Creating an account only takes a minute, and it’s worth it — not only do you unlock eligibility for titles, but you also gain access to streak rewards, badges, and other personalized features on the site.

A team win happens when multiple players from the same team perform well together in a KC Event. Here’s how it works:

  • In small team events: If two or more teammates finish consecutively on the leaderboard (for example, #1 and #2 or #2 and #3), they can all claim the Overall Winner title.
  • In larger team events (like Red vs Blue): If one team dominates the leaderboard (e.g., a majority of Red Team players make it to the top spots), that team earns a team victory. However, Overall Winner is still limited to players who hold those consecutive top positions (e.g., #1, #2, etc.).

This system rewards both individual excellence and team coordination. Whether you’re playing solo or with friends, aiming for those top positions is key.

Streaks are our way of rewarding players who stay active and connected with the KC community. Here’s how they work:

  • You earn streaks by logging in every day. The longer your streak, the more rewards you unlock.
  • At 10 days: You unlock a custom profile picture, letting you personalize your profile in a unique way.
  • At 50 days: You gain the ability to create a custom badge and access the Diamond Profile Customizer — a feature normally reserved for Diamond members of the community.
  • At 100 days: You unlock unlimited custom badge creation, giving you even more ways to express yourself, with more features planned for the future.

Streaks are a fun way to celebrate dedication. They’re designed not only to keep you engaged but also to give back to those who actively contribute to the community over time.

  • Account details: Username, email address, and profile settings.

  • Device data: Browser type, IP address, and basic technical info (for site security and performance).
  • Messages and interactions: Posts you make on our Message Board or during events.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Run events: Track scores, award badges, and manage leaderboards.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
